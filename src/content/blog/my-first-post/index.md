Hello world.

This is the beginning. A blank page that will eventually fill with writeups, research notes, and hard-won lessons from breaking things to understand how they work.

I'm Emmanuel — most people online know me as cocofelon. I break software for a living, but not in the way that makes lawyers nervous. I do it to make systems stronger, to understand the gaps defenders miss, and to document what I learn along the way.

## Why This Blog?

Writing is thinking made visible. When I write about a vulnerability, an evasion technique, or a methodology, I'm forced to understand it at a deeper level than if I just exploited it and moved on.

This blog will be:

- **Technical** — Real code, real techniques, real analysis
- **Honest** — Including the failures and dead ends
- **Evolving** — Growing as I grow

## What's Coming

Expect writeups on:

- Malware development and evasion research
- API and web application security
- Cloud exploitation and SSRF chains
- Tool development and automation
- CTF writeups and lab challenges

Some posts will be deep technical dives. Others will be quick notes on something interesting I found. All will be authentic.

**Let's go.**
